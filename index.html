📊 Highscore Search 🪙 Item Search

OSRS MultiTool

A quick and easy way to look up OldSchool RuneScape player highscores and item prices in real time.

🔍 Quick Guide

  1. Choose whether you want to search for a player or an item
  2. Select the chosen search feature and enter the name of the searched player or item
  3. Switch between the different search features in the upper left corner
